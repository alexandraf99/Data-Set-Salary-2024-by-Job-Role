# Data-Set-Salary-2024-by-Job-Role
The dataset I chose is Salary Insights by Job Role https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/zahranusrat/salary .

### Data findings
-The mean salary in US dollars of all the jobs in data fields would be 149,688.78. But the standard deviation is 68505, so there is variation and it could depend on different facts most likely, depends on the job title and experience. So to get a better analysis of what the mean is we can look at specific job titles and find a mean or median value.


### Analytical Question 1: What specific job titles within the data field makes the most in median salary
